  • Application to Vendor UMPSA – Completed: Registration with Universiti Malaysia Pahang Al-Sultan Abdullah (UMPSA) has been successfully completed. Our Vendor Reference No. is VA25-36913 under the company ASAVISION ENTERPRISE (Company Registration No. 202503118531 (RA0124306-V)). This registration enables us to participate in procurement and service opportunities offered by UMPSA.
  • Application to Kementerian Kewangan in Progress: Our application for registration with the Ministry of Finance (Kementerian Kewangan) is currently underway. This registration will enable us to participate in government tenders and expand our reach.

For Kementerian Kewangan Registration, follow these steps:

  1. Register business with SSM: Ensure your business is legally registered with the Companies Commission of Malaysia.
  2. Apply at ePerolehan portal: Submit your application through the official ePerolehan portal, the central procurement system for the Malaysian government.
  3. Upload vendor documentation and wait for approval: Provide all necessary supporting documents as required by the Ministry of Finance and await their official approval.
